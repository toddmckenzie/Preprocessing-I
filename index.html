Why I code?

The reason I started coding in April of 2018 was out of curiosity. I came to a period of my life where I became curious about many things that I did not know. Coding was not the only thing that I became curious about but it was the one thing I fell in love with doing. The reason code caught my attention so strong was because of the ability to make me struggle and think about a problem for awhile. I like to keep my mind focused on a problem and then the feeling I get when it gets solved is awesome.
